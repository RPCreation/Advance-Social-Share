== Description ==
Advance Social Share is a lightweight WordPress plugin that allows you to easily add social sharing icons to your posts. Share your content on popular social media platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Telegram, and Instagram. Customize the display of the social share buttons to match your website's style.

Features:
- Easily add social sharing icons to your posts.
- Share content on Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Telegram, and Instagram.
- Customize the display of social share buttons with custom CSS.

![Plugin Banner](https://example.com/path-to-banner-image.png)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `advance-social-share` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Customize the social share button styles in the plugin settings page.
4. Social share icons will be displayed on your single post pages automatically.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I customize the appearance of the social share buttons? =
You can customize the appearance of the social share buttons by adding custom CSS in the plugin settings.
